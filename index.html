About the Company

EPEA Consultants Africa Ltd (ECAL) is a consortium of solid and seasoned practitioners and consultants with a joint experience of over 60 years of providing practical HR solutions cutting through the Manufacturing, Agricultural, Logistics, Telecommunications, and Service sectors of the economy.

Their objective is to support businesses across the African region to apply appropriate people management strategies that enables best utilization of the people skills and competencies and provide an enabling work environment that leads to high performance and profitability of the units.

Our Areas of Specialization

In-depth expertise across key HR domains

Employee and Industrial Relations

We provide advisory services to help organizations manage union engagements, collective bargaining, and workplace relations. Our team assists in developing labor strategies, negotiating collective agreements, and resolving disputes in accordance with statutory requirements. We also advise on restructuring, redundancies, and grievance handling to maintain industrial harmony and operational stability.

HR Support Services

We offer comprehensive HR support across the employment lifecycle, including organizational reviews, recruitment and selection, performance management, and HR compliance audits. Our services also cover HR policy development, employment contracts, disciplinary and grievance management, employee separation advisory, exit interviews, career counseling, mentorship, and coaching.

Dispute & Conflict Resolutions

Our seasoned mediators partner with organizations to resolve complex disputes and facilitate constructive outcomes. We engage closely with all stakeholders to identify underlying interests and concerns, developing tailored solutions that ensure mutual satisfaction and preserve long-term relationships.

CBA and Contract Negotiations

Our specialist negotiators advise organizations on collective bargaining agreements and commercial contract negotiations. We work alongside management teams to prepare negotiation strategies, assess proposals, and secure favorable terms that balance organizational objectives with stakeholder interests.

Policy Advocacy

We provide strategic policy advisory services to help organizations engage with government and regulatory bodies. Our team assists in assessing the policy environment, developing advocacy strategies, preparing submissions, and facilitating stakeholder engagements to advance institutional objectives and ensure regulatory compliance.

Board Advisory Services

We provide specialized governance advisory to boards and governing bodies, including board evaluations, charter reviews, and policy formulation. Our services support director induction, succession planning, committee establishment, and role delineation between boards and management to ensure robust oversight and regulatory compliance.

Staff Training and Capacity Development

We design and deliver customized training programs to enhance institutional capacity and staff performance. Our services include needs assessments, competency-based training across technical and leadership areas, and post-training evaluation to ensure measurable skill application and sustained organizational impact.

Mediations and Conciliations

Our accredited mediators provide impartial dispute resolution services to help institutions resolve conflicts amicably. We facilitate structured dialogue between parties, identify underlying interests, and guide stakeholders toward mutually acceptable agreements while preserving professional relationships and ensuring confidentiality throughout the process.
